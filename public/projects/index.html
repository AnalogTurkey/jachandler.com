    Projects

    As of now I only have a handful of projects documented on this site. More to come.
    • Microphone Preamplifier Board

      • A preamplifier and power board I made for a stereo electret measurement microphone rig. This PCB is optimized for the Primo EM258 or Panasonic WM61 but may be used with others with some resistor value adjustments.
    • T.O.H.R.U (WIP)

      • The 'Testing Of Headphone Responses Unit' (TOHRU) is a stereo electret microphone rig used for acoustic measurements. Still a work in progress. Currently has a flat closed-cell foam coupler for headphone measurements.
    • Sony MDR-1000X Repair

      • Sony's MDR-1000X noise cancelling headphones have a minor design flaw, which means there's a plentiful supply of cheap and easily repairable broken high-end noise cancelling headphones on the used market.
    • Das Model S Mac Restoration

      • I found a Das Model S keyboard in bad shape at the campus surplus store and decided to fix it up (and take a look inside). Be warned, some of the pre-restoration images are pretty disgusting.
